Crêpes

Description

A dish that anyone can cook, and it goes well with either savory or sweet fillings

Ingredients (for 4 people, around 15 crêpes)

  • 2 eggs
  • 400 grams of AP flour
  • 200ml of sparkling water
  • 300ml regular milk
  • A pinch of salt (~2 grams)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Steps

  • With a whisking tool, mix up the liquid ingredients (water, milk, eggs, and oil)
  • To the mixed up liquid ingredients, slowly add the measured flour and mix well until the batter is free of clumps
  • Add the pinch of salt, and let the batter rest for ~15 minutes
  • In the meantime, heat up a pan with a bit of oil for the first, initial crêpe
  • Get a ladle, and add one ladle of batter to the pan, and fry up a crêpe. If needed, add more oil to the mix
  • Fill the crêpes with jam and/or chocolate cream and pack them tightly next to each other on a separate plate to keep them warm as possible
  • When everything is done, enjoy!
